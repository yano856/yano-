    Our Practicing

    This program includes Advanced Endurance and Technique, Speed and Sprints, and Mixed Distances and Pacing. This routine is designed to elevate your swimming skills while enhancing your overall fitness. As you progress, you’ll refine your technique and build stamina, allowing you to tackle more challenging workouts and improve your performance in the water!

    Endurance and Technique

    Focus on enhancing your swimming endurance and technique by incorporating targeted sets that challenge your stamina and refine your form. Engage in longer distance swims to build cardiovascular strength while integrating specific drills that emphasize efficient stroke mechanics and proper pacing. This combination will not only improve your overall endurance but also help you maintain excellent technique throughout your workouts, ensuring you swim with both power and efficiency.

    Speed and Sprints

    Incorporate speed and sprint sessions into your training, starting with short bursts of 25-50 meters at maximum effort, followed by adequate rest intervals. Begin with 4-6 sprints, focusing on explosive starts and maintaining proper technique throughout. As you gain confidence and strength, gradually increase the number of sprints and reduce rest time to enhance your speed and overall performance in the water.

    Mixed Distances and Pacing

    Incorporate mixed distance sets into your training, alternating between shorter sprints and longer swims to improve both speed and endurance. Start with 50-meter sprints followed by 200-meter swims at a steady pace, allowing for adequate rest in between. This approach will help you develop a versatile pacing strategy while enhancing your ability to maintain speed over varying distances, ultimately improving your overall swimming performance..

    Endurance and Techniques Day (Warm-Up,Main Set, Cool Down)

    01 - Warm-Up (5 minutes): P200m Freestyle (easy pace), 2 x 50m Technique Drills (catch-up, fingertip drag)


    02 - Main Set (15 minutes): 4 x 200m Freestyle (moderate pace, 20 seconds rest),4 x 50m Freestyle (build speed, 30 seconds rest)


    03 - Cool Down (5-10 minutes):200m Easy Swim (any stroke),100m Backstroke (gentle pace)


    Speed and Sprints Day (Warm-Up,Main Set ,Cool Down)

    01 -Warm-Up (5 minutes): 200m Freestyle (easy pace)


    02 - Main Set (15-20 minutes):6 x 50m Freestyle Sprints (max effort, 30 seconds rest),4 x 25m Butterfly Sprints (max effort, 30 seconds rest).


    03 - Cool Down (5 minutes): 200m Easy Swim (any stroke)


    Mixed Distances and Pacing Day (Warm-Up,Main Set ,Cool Down)

    01 - Warm-Up (5 minutes): 200m Freestyle (easy pace).


    02 - Main Set (20 minutes): 4 x 50m Freestyle (sprint, 30 seconds rest), 2 x 200m Freestyle (moderate pace, 20 seconds rest),4 x 100m IM (moderate pace, 15 seconds rest).


    03 - Cool Down (5 minutes): 200m Easy Swim (any stroke).
